Tweede

Overview

This project is a collaboration between Product Manager, UX Research, UX Design, and UI Design. This collaborative project was conducted using the Sprint Method which lasted for 6 weeks, sprint 1-3 on June 13-22. Our team was given a B2C/C2C Business Model project theme, and from the discussion it was decided that we chose the C2C Business Model.

The result of this project is a platform called Tweede. Tweede is a platform for buying and selling used goods, because currently in Indonesia there are also many people who buy and sell used goods to reduce the accumulation of useless goods or to become a business.
